Our Mission

Redefining waste, one creation at a time.

Thrift & Thrive is a bold student-led movement by OPG World School, where discarded materials are reborn as meaningful, sustainable creations.

We recycle and repurpose waste into high-quality, eco-friendly products.
Students lead the innovation with passion, creativity, and skill.
All proceeds go directly to charity, transforming trash into hope.

This isn't just about products — it's about purpose. By participating, you're helping the environment, uplifting lives, and inspiring change.
